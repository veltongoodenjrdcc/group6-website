FindYello.com Modernisation · AI Concierge Service Enhancement

Client Yello Media Group
Product FindYello.com
Horizon To Q2 2027

The Goal

Deliver the FindYello.com modernisation and launch the AI Concierge by Q2 2027, converting AI-assisted discovery into measurable advertiser leads.

Foundation
  • Mission: help Caribbean businesses be found, chosen, and contacted through modern digital discovery.
  • Vision: the region's trusted first stop for finding local businesses.
  • Values: verified accuracy, client independence, documented delivery, plain communication.
Competitive Advantages
  • Agent-assisted delivery workflow with senior project leadership.
  • Matrix structure pairing functional depth with one engagement lead.
  • Living project website as a public accountability record.
Financial 01
  • Grow advertiser lead events by 30% for the pilot cohort within two quarters of Concierge launch.
  • Deliver the engagement within the approved budget, with change requests costed before approval.
Customer / Client 02
  • Launch the AI Concierge with WhatsApp contact paths on verified listings.
  • Raise verified-listing coverage to 85% in pilot categories before launch.
  • Monthly plain-language performance summaries to every pilot advertiser.
Internal / Operational 03
  • Run continuous agentic listing verification with human confirmation on flags.
  • Hold 100% on-time weekly status reporting across all workstreams.
  • Route all scope changes through the change-control log.
People & Learning 04
  • Train every team member on agent-assisted workflows with human review.
  • Rotate backup reviewers across workstreams to spread knowledge.
  • Capture lessons at each milestone into the shared register.
The AI Service Enhancement

Agent 01

FindYello AI Concierge

Turns natural questions into verified matches with maps, reviews, and a WhatsApp contact path. Discovery becomes a conversation, not a keyword search.

Agent 02

Agentic listing verification

Continuously cross-checks phone numbers, hours, and websites, flagging stale records for human confirmation. Verification becomes continuous, not periodic.

Agent 03

Advertiser analytics agent

Turns raw lead events into a monthly plain-language summary for every SME advertiser: what happened, what changed, one next step.

Why it matters: the AI layer attacks the platform's two historic weaknesses at once, stale data and unclear advertiser value. Each agent strengthens the next.
