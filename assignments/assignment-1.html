Section 07 · Operations

Operational framework

The standing process Group 6 follows for every assignment this semester.

01

Review requirements together

The group reviews the assignment requirements as a team before any work begins.

02

Collect input early

Group input is gathered early using Google Forms, polls, and Telegram messages.

03

Assign tasks by strength

Tasks are assigned based on each member’s strengths, availability, and comfort level.

04

Set internal deadlines

Internal deadlines are set before the actual submission deadline.

05

Communicate on Telegram

Telegram is the main communication channel, with WhatsApp as backup.

06

Combine and review

All sections are combined into one document and given a final review.

07

Submit Moodle response + website URL

The final Moodle response is submitted along with the URL for the updated assignment section of this website.

Section 08 · Cadence

Meeting cadence & accountability

Group 6 will use Telegram as the main contact method for group updates. Based on the responses, the group will commit to a weekly check-in, with extra check-ins before major deadlines. Since availability varies for some members and Sunday was also identified as a possible meeting time, the group will use Telegram polls when necessary to confirm the most suitable time for each check-in.

Accountability protocol

  • The Leader/CEO or designated coordinator posts the check-in agenda.
  • Members provide updates on assigned tasks.
  • If a member cannot attend, they send an update before the meeting where possible.
  • Attendance and task progress are recorded.
  • Final submissions are reviewed for consistency, formatting, grammar, and completeness.
