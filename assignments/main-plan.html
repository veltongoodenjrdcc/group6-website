Ques 4 · Reporting

Organising the project for accurate, on-time reporting

Explain how you intend to organize the Project to facilitate accurate and on-time reporting.

Reporting follows the matrix structure: information moves along defined lines, on a fixed rhythm, in standard formats, from a single source of truth. Accuracy is engineered through verification habits, and timeliness through internal deadlines set ahead of external ones.

The Lines

Who reports to whom

Workstream leads report project progress to the Senior Manager, who consolidates into one engagement status for the CEO/CTO. The Senior Manager, as single point of contact, carries milestone reporting to the client-side liaison at Yello Media Group. One door in, one door out.

Mondays

Fifteen-minute stand-up

Each workstream answers three questions: what moved, what is at risk, what is needed. Both reporting axes hear the same answers at the same time, so conflicting instructions cannot accumulate across the week.

Fridays

Written workstream status

Each lead submits a short standard-format status: progress, risks, decisions needed, next steps, each item marked on-track, at-risk, or blocked. Standard formats make reports comparable week to week, which is what makes them accurate.

Monthly

Client steering review

The Senior Manager presents consolidated progress against milestones to the client, with decisions logged and change requests routed through the change-control process defined in Assignment 3.

Always

Single source of truth

A shared task register records every deliverable, owner, status, and deadline, with internal deadlines set 48 hours ahead of external ones to create buffer. Minutes and attendance are recorded for every meeting, a habit the group has kept since Assignment 2. This project website is the public face of that record, updated every assignment cycle.

Controls

Accuracy and escalation

Every figure in a report is checked against its source before inclusion, and every deliverable has a named backup reviewer. Anything at risk for more than 48 hours escalates to the Senior Manager, and unresolved items go to the CEO/CTO for a same-week decision.

Ques 5 · Artificial Intelligence

AI agents in the strategic management process

You intend to use Artificial Intelligence Agent(s) to assist with the strategic management process. Define artificial intelligence and briefly say what Ai agents are used for. Explain in practical terms how you intend to use it in your own business to make a difference.

Artificial intelligence is the capability of computer systems to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as understanding language, recognising patterns, learning from data, and making decisions. AI agents are software systems built on that capability which pursue goals with a degree of autonomy: they perceive context, plan multi-step actions, use tools such as search engines, databases, and messaging platforms, and complete tasks with limited human supervision. In business they are used for research and monitoring, customer service, workflow automation, data cleaning and verification, content production, and analytics summarisation.

Group 6 applies AI agents on two levels: inside its own consultancy operations, where an agent-assisted production workflow already runs, and inside the FindYello product itself, where the AI enhancement is the difference-maker.

Consultancy Ops 01

Agent-assisted production

Deliverables, build scripts, diagrams, and this website are produced through an agent-assisted workflow, with human review on every output. What once took the group days now takes hours, at higher formatting quality.

Consultancy Ops 02

Scanning and summarisation

Agents monitor market and competitor signals for the environmental scan and summarise meetings into minutes, feeding the Friday status reports defined in Ques 4.

Product 01

The FindYello AI Concierge

A conversational agent that turns natural questions, such as a request for a plumber in Portmore open now, into verified matches with maps, reviews, and a WhatsApp contact path. Discovery becomes a conversation instead of a keyword search.

Product 02

Agentic listing verification

Verification agents continuously cross-check phone numbers, opening hours, and websites, flagging stale records for human confirmation. Data quality is a directory's core asset, and agents make verification continuous instead of periodic.

Product 03

Advertiser analytics agent

Each month, an agent turns raw lead events into a plain-language performance summary for every SME advertiser: what happened, what changed, and one suggested next step.

The Difference

Why this matters

The AI layer attacks the platform's two historic weaknesses at once: stale data and unclear advertiser value. Verified data makes the Concierge trustworthy, and the Concierge generates the lead events the analytics agent reports. Each agent strengthens the next.
