Main Project Ques 6

The 50-mark centrepiece

Build out a one page Strategic Management Plan to target an objective/goal you have set for your startup and speak specifically to your Ai product/service enhancement.

Group 6 Answer

One goal, one page, AI threaded through

The plan targets a single goal: deliver the FindYello.com modernisation and launch the AI Concierge by Q2 2027, converting AI-assisted discovery into measurable advertiser leads. The AI enhancement runs through every perspective: the Concierge for consumers, agentic listing verification for data quality, and a monthly analytics agent for advertisers.

  • ✓Financial: advertiser lead events up 30% for the pilot cohort within two quarters of launch.
  • ✓Operational: 85% verified-listing coverage in pilot categories before the Concierge goes live.
  • ✓Rhythm: the Ques 4 reporting cadence keeps the plan measured weekly, monthly, and quarterly.
